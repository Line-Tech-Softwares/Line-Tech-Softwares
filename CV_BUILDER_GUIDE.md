# CV Builder - Complete Guide & Help

## 🎯 Quick Start Guide

### Step 1: Select Your Role
1. Click on **"Select Your Role/Profession"** dropdown at the top
2. Choose from 12+ predefined roles (Software Engineer, Product Manager, etc.)
3. Or select **"+ Add Custom Role"** to add your own position
4. **Get instant suggestions** for title, skills, and professional summary!

### Step 2: Fill Your Personal Details
1. **Upload Photo**: Click the camera icon to upload a professional headshot
2. **Full Name**: Enter your complete name
3. **Job Title**: Use the suggested title or customize it
4. **Contact Info**: Add email, phone, and location
5. **Professional Summary**: Write 2-3 lines about yourself (or use the suggested summary)

### Step 3: Add Your Experience
1. **Job Title**: Your position at the company
2. **Company Name**: Organization you worked for
3. **Dates**: Enter as "2020-2023" or "2 years"
4. **Description**: Key achievements and responsibilities
5. Click **"✓ Add Experience"** to add more positions

### Step 4: Add Your Education
1. **Degree/Qualification**: e.g., "Bachelor of Science in Computer Science"
2. **Institution**: University or training center name
3. **Year**: Graduation year or date range
4. Click **"✓ Add Education"** for additional qualifications

### Step 5: Add Skills & Languages
- **Skills**: Comma-separated list (JavaScript, Python, Project Management, etc.)
- **Languages**: Comma-separated with proficiency (English (Fluent), Spanish (Intermediate))

### Step 6: Export Your CV
- **Export as PDF**: Download directly to your device (works on iOS!)
- **Export as Image**: Save as PNG for social media sharing
- **Copy Preview**: Copy the formatted text to clipboard

**What are Credits?**

**How it Works**:
1. Start each day with 10 credits
2. Export CVs, images, or documents as needed
3. When you run out, you can export tomorrow with your fresh 10 credits

**Status Indicators**:

- ⚠️ **Yellow**: 1-3 credits remaining (use wisely)

## 💡 Pro Tips

### 1. Use Role Suggestions
- Your selected role automatically populates suggested text
- Mix suggestions with your personal experience
- Customize to match your specific background

### 2. Skills Format
✅ **Good format**: "JavaScript, React, Node.js, Python, AWS, Docker, Git"
✅ **With levels**: "JavaScript (Expert), Python (Intermediate), AWS (Beginner)"

### 3. Professional Summary
A good summary:
- Starts with years of experience
- Highlights key achievements
- Mentions your specialization
- Shows career goals

**Example**:
> "Experienced Full-Stack Developer with 5+ years building scalable web applications using React and Node.js. Passionate about clean code and mentoring junior developers. Proven track record of delivering projects 20% faster than deadlines."

### 4. Work Experience
**Strong description**:
- Use action verbs (Developed, Led, Managed, Achieved)
- Include metrics when possible (20% improvement, $500K saved)
- Focus on impact, not just tasks
- Keep to 2-3 bullet points per position

### 5. Dates Format
Go with what works:
- ✅ "2020-2023"
- ✅ "January 2020 - December 2023"
- ✅ "5 years"
- ✅ "Recently"

### 6. Photo Tips
- Use a professional headshot
- Clear, well-lit background
- Formal business attire
- Looking at camera
- JPG or PNG 100x100px minimum

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Will my data be saved?
**A**: Your data is currently saved in your browser's local storage. For permanent backup, export your CV as PDF or image.

### Q: Can I edit after exporting?
**A**: Yes! Your data stays in the form. Make changes and re-export. Each export uses 1 credit.

### Q: What if my CV spans multiple pages?
**A**: The PDF automatically handles multiple pages. Your entire CV will be included.

### Q: Can I use this on mobile?
**A**: Absolutely! The CV builder is fully optimized for mobile devices. Upload photos, fill forms, and export directly from your phone or tablet.

### Q: Why can't I download the PDF on iOS?
**A**: This is now fixed! Our new system detects iOS and uses special handling to ensure PDFs download properly instead of just previewing.

### Q: Can I change the CV layout/design?
**A**: Currently, we provide a professional single-column layout. Future updates will include multiple layout options.

### Q: Can I import a CV from another tool?
**A**: Not yet, but you can manually copy-paste your information. We're working on import functionality.

### Q: Is there a limit to how many sections I can add?
**A**: No limit! Add as many experiences, educations, or skills as you need.

### Q: Can I delete my CV data?
**A**: Simply clear your browser's cache for this site, or use your browser's "Clear Data" option. **Warning**: This will delete your data permanently.

## 🎓 Common CV Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Too long**: Keep to 1-2 pages (hiring managers spend 30 seconds on each CV)
❌ **Generic**: Customize for each job application
❌ **Spelling errors**: Proofread carefully before exporting
❌ **No metrics**: Add numbers, percentages, achievements
❌ **Poor formatting**: We handle this, but keep descriptions concise
❌ **Missing keywords**: Include skills from the job description
❌ **Outdated info**: Keep software and certifications current
❌ **No summary**: Professional summary sets the tone
